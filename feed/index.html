🎯 Pro Feeding Tips & Strategies

🍽️ Efficient Feeding Strategy

Budget Option: Use Potatoes and Tomatoes for consistent feeding at low cost.

Premium Option: Pineapples and Mangoes give best hunger-to-cost ratio for high-tier pets.

Mutation Feeding: Save Golden and Rainbow fruits for your most valuable pets!

⏰ Hunger Management

Timing: Pets lose hunger over time - feed regularly to maintain abilities.

Emergency: Keep some fast-growing crops like Lettuce for quick hunger fixes.

Optimization: Feed pets right before they use their abilities for maximum efficiency.

💰 Cost-Effective Feeding

Early Game: Carrots and Lettuce provide decent hunger at minimal cost.

Mid Game: Strawberries and Potatoes offer good value proposition.

End Game: Use mutation fruits to maximize both feeding and profit.

🧬 Mutation Synergy

Dragonfly: Creates Golden fruits - feed these to other pets for massive hunger restoration.

Polar Bear: Frozen fruits last longer and provide more nutrition.

Disco Bee: Disco fruits are the ultimate pet food - save for S-tier pets!

🎯 Pet-Specific Tips

Bunnies: Don't manually feed carrots - they eat them automatically for bonus value!

Caterpillars: Feed leafy fruits like Lettuce for bonus nutrition absorption.

Bug Pets: Prefer earthy foods like Mushrooms and Potatoes.

📊 Advanced Strategies

Batch Feeding: Grow multiple crops at once for efficient pet feeding sessions.

Mutation Farming: Use mutation pets to create premium feeding materials.

Profit Loop: Sell normal fruits, keep mutations for feeding premium pets.
